Overview

Site Purpose

This site will help college students learn how to manage their money by tracking income and expenses. It includes a simple budget calculator and student-friendly financial tips to encourage smart spending habits.

Audience

The target audience is college students, especially those with part-time jobs or limited income, looking for an easy way to plan a budget. Married students or students new to financial independence will also benefit.

Dynamic Elements

JavaScript will be used to create an interactive budget calculator. Users will input their income and expense categories, and the script will calculate their leftover balance. Depending on the result, a message will appear to encourage or warn the user. A rotating financial tip or quote will display on the home page every time it loads.

Logo

I created this simple logo using Canva. It includes a dollar sign and a graduation cap to represent financial success in school.

Content

Home Page

Welcome to College Budget Helper! We make budgeting simple for college students. Learn how to track your income, manage your expenses, and set financial goals—all in one place.

You’ll also get weekly budgeting tips and inspirational quotes to stay motivated on your journey to financial peace.

Budget Calculator Page

Input your monthly income and spending amounts for rent, food, school, and more. We’ll calculate your budget balance and let you know if you’re within budget or overspending.
